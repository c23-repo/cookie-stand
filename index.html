Sunday Funday

Join us on the second Sunday of every month to make your own salmon cookies! Family, friends, and teams all have a great time making their own cookies. At the end of the event, the best decorated cookie wins a prize!

About Us

Originally created as a personal snack, our organic, sugar free, and high protein cookies became an instant sensation between friends and family. Our mission was to make the perfect confection to be paired with a cup of coffee in the morning. As a family owned and operated business, Pat's Salmon Cookies is deeply rooted in the community, giving back a portion of every cookie sold to the less fortunate. So enjoy this delcious and nutricious way to start your day, while making a difference in your community.
